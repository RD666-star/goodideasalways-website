## What is RunBeat?

RunBeat is a simple yet powerful running cadence metronome. Set your target cadence, put on your headphones, and let the beat guide your steps.

## Features

- Adjustable BPM for any pace
- Clean, distraction-free interface
- Works with your music
- Battery-friendly design
